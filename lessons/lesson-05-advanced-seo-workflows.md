## Hands-on Activity/Assignment

**Assignment: Comprehensive SEO Content Campaign**

Develop and execute a complete 30-day SEO content campaign using Writesonic. This assignment simulates a real-world scenario where you need to plan, create, and optimize content at scale.

**Part 1: Campaign Strategy Document (600-800 words)**
Create a detailed campaign plan that includes:
- Target audience analysis
- Keyword research (15-20 keywords)
- Competitive analysis (3-5 competitors)
- Content calendar for 30 days (minimum 12 content pieces)
- Success metrics and KPIs
- Resource allocation plan

**Part 2: Competitive Gap Analysis (400-500 words)**
Analyze your top 3 competitors:
- Content themes they're covering
- Keywords they rank for that you don't
- Content gaps and opportunities
- Quality assessment of their content
- Your differentiation strategy

**Part 3: Content Creation (Create 5 pieces)**
Using Writesonic, create a diverse content portfolio:
1. **1 Pillar Page** (2,000+ words) - comprehensive guide on your main topic
2. **2 Cluster Articles** (1,000-1,500 words each) - supporting content
3. **1 FAQ/List Article** (800-1,000 words) - quick-answer format
4. **1 Comparison/Review Article** (1,000-1,200 words) - product/service comparison

Each piece should include:
- SEO-optimized title and meta description
- Proper heading structure
- Internal linking plan
- External citations (where appropriate)
- Target keyword integration

**Part 4: Optimization Report (500-600 words)**
Document your process:
- How you used Writesonic for each content type
- SEO scores and optimization techniques applied
- Challenges encountered and solutions
- Quality assurance checklist
- Next steps for campaign continuation

**Part 5: Performance Projection (300-400 words)**
Project expected outcomes:
- Ranking timelines for target keywords
- Traffic projections (be realistic)
- Conversion expectations
- How you'll measure success
- Iteration plan based on performance data

**Deliverables:**
- Campaign strategy document
- Competitive gap analysis
- 5 completed content pieces
- Optimization report
- Performance projection plan
